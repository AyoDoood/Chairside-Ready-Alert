What it does

Chairside Ready Alert is a simple LAN messaging tool for dental practices. With one click, a clinical assistant can let the doctor know "Room 2 is ready" — the doctor's PC pings, blinks, and brings the window to the foreground. No server. No cloud account. No extra hardware.

Every workstation runs the same app. Each one is given a station label (Room 1, Room 2, Doctor, Lab, etc.). The app discovers the others on your local network automatically and shows them in a one-tap send list. That's it.

Features

  • One-click "Ready" alerts between rooms
  • Zero-configuration peer discovery
  • Per-workstation station labels
  • 15 built-in alert sounds
  • Per-recipient sound choice
  • Per-workstation volume control
  • Default-targets for one-tap multi-send
  • Three built-in visual themes
  • System tray quick-send
  • Local connection log for troubleshooting
  • Manual peer IP fallback
  • Up to 10 devices per Microsoft account

Privacy & ownership

Your data stays in your office. Chairside Ready Alert does not contact any cloud service, send analytics, or transmit data outside your local network. Station labels and alert messages travel only between devices on the same LAN.

The app makes no outbound internet calls during normal operation. Updates are delivered through the Microsoft Store; that is the only network dependency, and only while the Store update check is running.
